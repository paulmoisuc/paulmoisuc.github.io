My story

My name is Paul, I am from Romania and I’m 23 years old. I have more than 7 years experience in editing videos. It all started in 2014 when I have joined YouTube as a hobby, I have created and edited more than 350 videos now, from simple YouTube videos such as gaming montages and vlogs to car cinematics and a lot more.

I just graduated from the Faculty of Electrical, Energy and Applied Computer Engineering but I have to take the last exams.

In my free time, I am a organizer of car events in a car community in the county where I belong and I make the official cinematics of the events we make.
